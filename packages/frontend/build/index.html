Who We Are

The Sustainability Cooperative at the University of Maryland, fondly known as SCoop, is a collective of student organizations interested in improving sustainability on campus. Founded by students in 2018, SCoop continues to grow and host more opportunities for members of our community and for the larger university and city. By joining together, we optimize funding opportunities, and individual SCoop member organizations gain an advantage in building interdisciplinary project teams. The cooperative meets regularly to network and workshop concepts and projects, allowing for increased collaboration and innovation as well as elevated awareness of local initiatives!

What We Do

Cleanups

Sometimes partnering with We Are Water Collective and sometimes setting up our own hikes, we regularly host opportunities to explore and clean up nature with our fellow SCoop-ers!

Workshops

At our biweekly meetings, we offer the opportunity for any individual or organization to submit proposals for collaborative workshops focused on specific projects. Submit your workshop proposal here and join us at our meetings in the ESJ loft to participate in others!

Networking

SCoop meetings and events give members a chance to meet other individuals and organizations in the local sustainability community and find opportunities for jobs and involvement in projects.
